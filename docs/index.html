Case Study: Sales Performance Optimization

S

Situation

I analyzed 4 years of global e-commerce sales data across 13 regions and 3 product categories, simulating the role of a PM identifying performance gaps and growth opportunities.

T

Task

I analyzed 51,290 transactions across 1,590 customers and did the following:

  • Identify the region with the highest uplift potential
  • Calculate key PM metrics: MRR growth, CAC trends, and profit margins
  • Determine root causes of underperformance at category level
  • Propose data-driven product features to close performance gaps
A

Action

I built a comprehensive Python analytics pipeline to transform raw sales data into actionable insights:

1. Data Cleaning & Quality Assurance

Handled missing values using category-based median imputation, standardized date formats, and validated data integrity across all dimensions.

2. Metric Calculation

Computed MRR growth (10.2%), CAC reduction (61.3%), and regional profit margins across 13 global markets.

3. Root Cause Analysis

Identified Southeast Asia as the primary opportunity with negative Furniture margins (-2.3%) compared to Canada's 26.6% margin.

4. Interactive Visualization

Created four interactive dashboards: sales trends, regional performance, category distribution, and customer segmentation analysis.

R

Result

This analysis identified clear opportunities for revenue optimization. Key recommendation: Address regional pricing inconsistencies, particularly in Southeast Asia where Furniture margins are negative.
