## üí° Key Findings & Insights

### 1. Business Health Metrics
- **Monthly Revenue Growth (MRR):** 10.2% - Consistent upward trajectory
- **Total Profit:** $1.47 million across 4 years
- **CAC Reduction:** 61.3% improvement in customer acquisition efficiency

### 2. Critical Problem Identified
**Southeast Asia Furniture Segment:**
- **Margin:** -2.3% (NEGATIVE)
- **Benchmark:** Canada achieves 26.6% margin in same category
- **Gap:** 24.6 percentage points difference
- **Impact:** Major profit leakage in growing Asian market

### 3. Revenue Opportunity
**Quantified Potential:** $217,000 incremental profit (14.8% increase)
- Fix Southeast Asia Furniture pricing/cost structure
- Align with top-performing region benchmarks
- Apply regional optimization strategies

### 4. Customer Segmentation Insights
**Power Users Analysis:**
- **Top 20% of customers** drive **43.5% of total revenue**
- **Average revenue per power user:** $17,000 vs $5,600 (standard customers)
- **Margin performance:** 12% vs 11.3% (power users more profitable)
- **Strategic implication:** Focus retention efforts on high-frequency segment

### 5. Category Performance
**Technology:** Highest profit contributor ($663K)  
**Office Supplies:** Steady volume leader  
**Furniture:** Mixed performance - strong in some regions, negative in others

### Key Insight #1: Regional Pricing Gap
"The 24.6% margin gap between Southeast Asia and Canada in Furniture indicates either pricing is too low, costs are too high, or discount strategy needs adjustment in the underperforming region."

### Key Insight #2: Power User Economics
"20% of customers generate 43.5% of revenue. These power users spend 3x more ($17K vs $5.6K) and maintain higher margins. Retention strategy should prioritize this segment."

### The Recommendations
"Four actionable strategies emerged:
1. **Regional pricing strategy** - Address Southeast Asia's negative furniture margins through price optimization or cost reduction
2. **Power user focus** - Build retention programs for high-frequency customers
3. **Category-specific optimization** - Leverage Technology's strong performance ($663K profit)
4. **Supply chain review** - Investigate why Southeast Asia costs are 24% higher than benchmarks"

### The Business Impact
